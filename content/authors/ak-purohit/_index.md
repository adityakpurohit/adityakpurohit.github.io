Dr. Aditya Kumar Purohit is a human–computer interaction researcher whose work explores how technology can be designed to foster healthier, more trustworthy digital experiences. He is a Senior Research Scientist at the Center for Advanced Internet Studies (CAIS) in Bochum, Germany, where he studies communication platforms, the impact of large language models on user interaction and society, and digital interventions that encourage mindful technology use.
